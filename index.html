About Me

From Patient Care

I spent years as a Patient Care Coordinator, the person fielding calls at 8 a.m., chasing insurance approvals, rescheduling no-shows, and watching talented clinicians drown in paperwork instead of caring for patients.

I knew the problem intimately: healthcare clinics are extraordinary at medicine but buried in administration. So I made a deliberate pivot — deeply investing in AI and automation, determined to solve the exact pain points I'd lived firsthand.

Today I bridge two worlds most consultants can't. I understand clinical workflows, patient psychology, and compliance requirements, and I know exactly how to automate them without disrupting care quality or confusing your team.

This isn't about replacing people. It's about freeing them to do what they do best: heal.
