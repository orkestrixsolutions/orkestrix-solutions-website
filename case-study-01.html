Client Snapshot

Practice Type

Multi-specialty outpatient (primary care + behavioral health)

Size

4 providers · 2 locations · ~280 appts/week

EHR

Athenahealth

Before

Front desk manually called no-shows — 2 hrs/day, inconsistent, no tracking

Stack

n8n (self-hosted AWS EC2) · Twilio SMS · Gmail · Athenahealth webhook · Airtable · Slack

The Problem

  • 18–22% no-show rate with no recovery process

  • 10 hrs/week manual callbacks with no consistency

  • No outreach documentation — significant compliance risk

  • High-risk patients missing critical follow-ups undetected

  • No data on what outreach methods actually worked

The Solution

  • Automated 4-touch sequence triggered on EHR no-show tag

  • PHI encrypted at field level before workflow processing

  • Risk scoring routes high-acuity patients to provider alert

  • AI sentiment scanning stops outreach on distress signals

  • All outcomes logged to Airtable for full audit trail

Automation Sequence

D0

Immediate · Twilio SMS

"We missed you today."

"We missed you today — here's a quick link to find a time that works."

D1

Day 1 · Gmail Email

"Your care team is still here."

"We know life gets busy — your care team is still here when you're ready."

D3

Day 3 · SMS + Slack Alert

"We saved your spot."

"We saved your spot — it only takes 60 seconds to rebook." + Front desk Slack notification.

D7

Day 7 · Email + Portal + Phone Task

"This is our last note."

"This is our last note — we'd still love to see you." Auto phone task assigned to staff for unresponsive patients.

Stop Conditions

✗

STOP Reply

Patient sends STOP — all outreach halted immediately and opt-out logged.

✓

Appointment Booked

EHR confirms new appointment — sequence ends, outcome recorded.

⚑

Care Cancellation

Provider marks care cancelled — outreach stops and patient is flagged.

⚠

Distress Language

AI sentiment scan detects distress signals — escalated to clinical staff immediately.

⊘

Opted-Out Channel

Patient opt-out on that channel — message skipped, alternate channel tried.

↻

Human Escalation

After D7 with no response — phone task auto-assigned to front desk staff.

Results

62%

Touch 1 Recoveries

More than half of all recovered patients re-booked at the very first SMS touchpoint on Day 0.

23%

Touch 2–3 Recoveries

Another 23% of recovered patients responded to the Day 1 email or Day 3 reminder sequence.

8 hrs

Weekly Staff Time Freed

Front desk reclaimed 8 hours per week previously spent on manual no-show callback calls.

Compliance & Privacy

All PHI is encrypted at field level before workflow processing. Outreach is TCPA-compliant via channel opt-in checks, time-zone gating, and immediate STOP suppression. Every workflow run is logged to CloudTrail for audit purposes. No PHI is transmitted through non-HIPAA-eligible services.
