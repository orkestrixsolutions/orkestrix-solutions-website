Patient Experience

From First Click to Confirmed Appointment

What your new patient experiences — and what happens automatically behind the scenes.

What the Patient Sees
1
Lands on your website or portal

Clicks "Book an Appointment" — available on any device, any time of day.

2
Completes smart intake form

Answers adaptive questions (only what's relevant to their reason for visit), uploads insurance card, signs consent forms.

3
Selects from available slots

Matched to the right provider and location based on their responses. Picks a time that works for them.

4
Receives instant confirmation

SMS + email confirmation with appointment details, directions, and what to bring. Done in under 6 minutes.

What Happens Automatically
Patient record created in EHR

All intake data maps directly into your EHR fields — no manual re-entry, no transcription errors.

Insurance verification triggered

Insurance details are verified automatically within minutes of submission — before the patient even walks in.

Appointment added to provider schedule

Slot is blocked in the provider's calendar and the appointment type is set based on the patient's intake responses.

Reminder sequence activated

The no-show prevention sequence begins automatically — no separate setup needed for each new patient.

Intelligent Intake Form Features

Adaptive Logic

Questions change based on previous answers. A patient coming in for back pain sees different fields than one visiting for anxiety — reducing completion time and abandonment.

Insurance Card Capture

Patients photograph their insurance card on mobile — our system extracts the member ID, group number, and payer name automatically via OCR.

Digital Consent & HIPAA Notices

Consent forms and HIPAA notices are signed digitally within the intake flow. All signed documents are stored in the patient record with timestamp and IP log.

Provider Matching

The system recommends the most appropriate provider based on the patient's chief complaint, insurance, location preference, and provider availability.

Multi-Language Support

Intake forms available in English, Spanish, and additional languages based on your patient population — reducing barriers for non-English-speaking patients.

All Data Is
End-to-End Encrypted

Every form submission, document, and image is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Full HIPAA compliance documented.
