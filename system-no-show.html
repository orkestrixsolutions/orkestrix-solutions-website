The 4-Touch Reminder Sequence

1

Booking Confirmation

Immediately

Automated confirmation sent via email + SMS the moment an appointment is booked. Includes date, time, provider, and any pre-visit instructions.

2

72-Hour Reminder

3 Days Before

Friendly reminder with a one-tap confirm or reschedule link. AI sentiment scanning detects reply tone and flags uncertain responses for staff review.

3

Day-Before Reminder

24 Hours Before

Final reminder with directions, parking info, and what to bring. Unconfirmed appointments are automatically flagged and the slot is placed on a standby waitlist.

4

No-Show Recovery

Post-Appointment

If a patient misses their appointment, an automated recovery message sends within 2 hours offering easy rescheduling and the next 3 available slots.

System Capabilities

EHR-Triggered Automation

Sequences start automatically when appointments are booked — no manual setup required per patient.

Intelligent Waitlist Management

Cancelled slots are instantly offered to patients on a dynamic waitlist, sorted by urgency and availability match.

Multi-Channel Outreach

SMS, email, and optional automated voice calls — each patient's preferred channel is learned and prioritized over time.

AI Sentiment Scanning

Patient replies are analyzed in real time. Confused, hesitant, or urgent responses are flagged for a staff member to follow up personally.

No-Show Rate Dashboard

Weekly reports showing no-show rate trends, recovery rate, revenue recovered, and which message touchpoints are most effective.

Full HIPAA Audit Trail

Every outreach message, patient reply, and scheduling action is logged with timestamps for complete compliance documentation.
